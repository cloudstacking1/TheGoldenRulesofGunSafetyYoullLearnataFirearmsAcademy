A CCW (Concealed Carry Weapon) Permit Class is a training course designed to educate individuals on the legal, ethical, and practical aspects of carrying a concealed firearm. The curriculum typically includes firearm safety, local and state laws regarding concealed carry, situational awareness, and defensive tactics. Successful completion of the class often leads to certification, which is required to apply for a concealed carry permit in many jurisdictions.

The duration of a Concealed Carry Permit Class can vary depending on the state and the specific requirements of the training provider. Generally, classes range from 4 to 8 hours and may include both classroom instruction and live-fire training. It's essential to check with your local training academy to understand the specific duration and structure of their courses.
